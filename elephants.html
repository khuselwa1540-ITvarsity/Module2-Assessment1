ELEPHANTS

Elephants are large mammals of the family Elephantide and the other Proboscidea.Too traditionally recognised, the African elephant (Loxodonta africana ) and the Asian are scattered throughout sub-saharan Africa, South Asian.

All elephants have several distinctive feartures the most notable of which is a long proboscis, used for many purposes, particularly breathing, lifting water and grasping objects.

Elephants are herbivours and can be found in different habits including savannah forsts and deserts as well as marshes.
