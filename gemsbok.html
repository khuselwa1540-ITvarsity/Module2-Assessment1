GEMSBOK

Gemsbok or gemsbuck(Oryz gazella) is a large antelope in the arid regions of Southern Africa, such as Kalahari Desert. Some authorities formaly found as subspecies.

The gemsbuck is depicted on the coat of the arms of the Namibia, where all speceies is estimated at 373,000 individuals.

The name "gemsbok in English is derived from Afrikaans gemsbok Dutch name of the male chamois,gemsbok. Although some superficial similarities app are notices, the chamios and the oryx are not closely related.
