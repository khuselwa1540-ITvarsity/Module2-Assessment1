GIRAFFE

Giraffes are the tallest land animals. A Giraffe could look into a second-story window without even having to stand on its tiptoes.

Giraffes are so big that they really don't need to hide from predators. There is safety in number! Its hard to pick one giraffe from another when they form a tight group.

Its easy to understand why giraffes top the list of so many people's wildlife. Their elegant stride, outrageous eyelashes, and can calm expression give them an air of refinement.
