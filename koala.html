KOALA

Koalas are well-known for their large round head, big furry ears and big black nose.

Their fur is usually grey-brown in colour with white fur on the chest, inner arms, ears and bottom.

Even though there are a primary symbol of Australia, Koals do not live across the entire continent. These mammals primarly live along the eastern coast of Australia. Their range include much of Queensland, New South Wales and Victoria
