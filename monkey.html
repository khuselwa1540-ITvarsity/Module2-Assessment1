MONKEY

Monkey are clever, social animals. They are known for running and leaping through trees with ease.

Monkey look like apes such as chimpanzes, orangutans and gorillas. But monkeys differ from apes in several ways. For instance, every monkey has a tail, even if it is just a tiny nub.Monkeys also generally have narrow chests than apes do.

Monkeys belong to the group of mammals called primates.
