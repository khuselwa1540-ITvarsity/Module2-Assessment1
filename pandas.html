PANDAS

Pandas have a distinctive black and white coat, with black fur around their eyes and on their ears, muzzles, legs and shoulders. Their thick, wooly coat helps to keep them warm in their cool mountain homes. An adult panda can weigh up to 150kg, with male 10% larger and 20% heavier than females

They have great camouflage for their environment. They spend alot of their day eating.

Pandas have carnivous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit
