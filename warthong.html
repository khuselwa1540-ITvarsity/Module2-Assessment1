WARTHONGS

Warthongs are plump, hoved animals with large nostrils at the end of the snout. They have little fur, except for a mane that goes the spine to the middle of the back, according to the Animal Diversity Web(ADW)

Warthongs are found in open and lightly forested areas of Africa.

The warthongs is a sparsely haired, large-headed, blackish or brown animal standing aout 76cm at a shoulder.
